== Description ==

This is a plug-in for a web shop to maintain it’s opening and closing time for different days of a week. Isn’t this awesome ?

The Key Feature of this awesome plugin are given below:

1. It will manage your shop’s opening and closing time for several days. And you can easily change your scheduling for different days.
2. It will restrict your customers to make order while your web-shop is not running. (Pro-Feature).
3. You can show your scheduling for 7 days anywhere you want like in a page or in a sidebar, or in a post etc simply using a short-code.
4. Your Customers will able to know weather the web-shop is open or close. Also they will know the opening and closing time of that day.
5. It is supported with all versions of WordPress and Woocommerce.

So Have a copy of WOC Open Close plugin and make your web-shop more smart.

Thanks.
